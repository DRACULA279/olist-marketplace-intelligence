# Business Requirements Document (BRD)

# Project Title

Olist E-Commerce Analytics & Executive Intelligence Platform

---

# 1. Business Problem

The Olist marketplace operates across multiple product categories, sellers, and customer segments. As marketplace scale increases, operational complexity also increases across logistics, customer satisfaction, delivery performance, and seller quality management.

The business currently lacks a centralized analytical framework to monitor:

* Revenue growth trends
* Operational performance
* Delivery efficiency
* Customer satisfaction
* Product-category performance
* Seller contribution and quality

Without integrated analytics, identifying operational bottlenecks and strategic growth opportunities becomes difficult.

---

# 2. Business Objectives

The primary objectives of this project are:

* Analyze marketplace revenue performance and growth patterns.
* Identify top-performing product categories and revenue drivers.
* Evaluate customer satisfaction trends and operational pain points.
* Measure delivery performance and logistics efficiency.
* Assess seller contribution and operational quality.
* Build an executive dashboard for centralized KPI monitoring and decision-making.

---

# 3. Stakeholders

The following stakeholders would benefit from the analytical solution:

| Stakeholder              | Business Need                            |
| ------------------------ | ---------------------------------------- |
| Executive Leadership     | Marketplace health and growth visibility |
| Operations Team          | Delivery and logistics monitoring        |
| Product Team             | Product-category performance insights    |
| Customer Experience Team | Customer satisfaction monitoring         |
| Seller Management Team   | Seller quality and contribution analysis |

# 4. Scope of Analysis

The project covers the following business domains:

* Revenue Analytics
* Customer Analytics
* Delivery Analytics
* Review Analytics
* Product Analytics
* Seller Analytics

---

# 5. Key Business Questions

The project aims to answer the following questions:

* Which product categories drive the highest revenue?
* How does delivery performance impact customer reviews?
* Which operational issues generate customer dissatisfaction?
* Which categories behave as premium segments?
* How concentrated is marketplace revenue?
* Which sellers contribute the most operational value?

---

# 6. Success Criteria

The project will be considered successful if it enables stakeholders to:

* Monitor marketplace KPIs centrally.
* Identify operational bottlenecks quickly.
* Track customer satisfaction trends.
* Analyze category-level revenue performance.
* Evaluate delivery efficiency.
* Support data-driven operational and strategic decisions.

# 9. Assumptions & Constraints

## Assumptions

* Dataset accurately represents marketplace transactions.
* Review scores reflect genuine customer sentiment.
* Delivery timestamps are operationally reliable.

## Constraints

* Limited NLP analysis for multilingual reviews.
* Marketplace data limited to available dataset period.
* Real-time refresh pipelines not implemented.

---

# 10. Expected Business Impact

The analytical framework can help stakeholders:

* Improve logistics efficiency
* Monitor customer satisfaction
* Optimize seller performance
* Identify high-value product categories
* Support strategic marketplace decision-making

The project demonstrates how integrated analytics and business intelligence systems can improve operational visibility across e-commerce ecosystems.
