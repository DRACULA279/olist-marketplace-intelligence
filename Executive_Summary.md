# Executive Summary

## Overview

This project analyzes the Brazilian Olist e-commerce marketplace to evaluate overall marketplace health, operational efficiency, customer satisfaction, revenue performance, and product-category dynamics.

The analysis was conducted using PostgreSQL, Python, and Power BI to simulate a real-world business intelligence workflow used in modern analytics teams.

The primary objective was to identify operational bottlenecks, revenue-driving segments, customer satisfaction patterns, and strategic improvement opportunities across the marketplace ecosystem.

---

# Business Performance Summary

The marketplace demonstrated strong overall commercial performance with approximately:

- 13.59M total revenue
- 99K total orders
- 96K total customers
- 136.68 average order value
- 4.09 average review score
- 93.43% on-time delivery rate

Revenue and order volume showed consistent growth over time, indicating healthy marketplace expansion and increasing customer adoption.

---

# Key Findings

## 1. Delivery Performance Strongly Influences Customer Satisfaction

One of the strongest patterns identified during the analysis was the relationship between delivery delays and customer dissatisfaction.

Orders delivered late consistently received significantly lower review scores compared to on-time deliveries, indicating that logistics performance is one of the primary drivers of customer experience within the marketplace.

This suggests that improving delivery consistency could generate disproportionate improvements in customer satisfaction and retention.

---

## 2. Revenue Concentration Exists Across Key Categories

A small group of product categories contributed a large portion of total marketplace revenue.

Top-performing categories included:

- Health Beauty
- Watches Gifts
- Bed Bath Table
- Sports Leisure
- Computers Accessories

These categories acted as the primary commercial growth drivers for the marketplace.

---

## 3. Premium Categories Display Different Pricing Behaviors

Product analysis revealed that some categories demonstrated stable premium pricing structures, while others appeared heavily influenced by pricing outliers.

Categories such as computers and specialized appliances maintained consistently high pricing patterns, whereas some broader categories combined mass-market and premium products within the same segment.

This distinction is important for pricing strategy, inventory planning, and category positioning.

---

## 4. Customer Reviews Remain Largely Positive

Despite operational inefficiencies in certain areas, customer reviews remained heavily skewed toward positive ratings.

Review score analysis showed strong concentration around 4-star and 5-star reviews, suggesting that overall marketplace trust and customer perception remained healthy.

However, negative reviews were strongly concentrated around operational failures such as:

- Delivery delays
- Damaged products
- Missing items
- Customer service issues

---

## 5. Review Categorization Has Further Analytical Potential

Initial review categorization identified logistics-related complaints as a major source of dissatisfaction.

However, a large percentage of reviews remained grouped under generalized categories due to limitations in manual keyword-based classification.

Future improvements using NLP-based sentiment analysis and text classification could significantly improve complaint detection and operational intelligence.

---

# Strategic Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following business recommendations were identified:

- Prioritize logistics optimization to reduce delivery delays and improve customer satisfaction.
- Focus operational monitoring on high-revenue categories to protect core marketplace revenue streams.
- Implement deeper seller-quality monitoring for categories with higher complaint concentration.
- Expand NLP-driven review analysis for more accurate customer feedback classification.
- Develop forecasting and anomaly-detection systems for operational monitoring.

---

# Conclusion

The marketplace demonstrated strong overall growth and healthy customer sentiment, but operational consistency — particularly around logistics performance — emerged as a critical factor influencing customer experience.

The project highlights how integrated SQL analytics, operational KPI tracking, and business intelligence dashboards can support data-driven decision-making across marketplace ecosystems.
